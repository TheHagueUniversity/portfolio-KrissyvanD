Work Experience

Albert Heijn
April 2018 - Current

Since April 2018 I've been working at the Albert Heijn as cashier B (regular check-out and self check-out). While working as a cashier, I have a lot of contact with costumers doing their groceries. This means there is a lot of opportunity to talk and brighten up someone's day. Another thing I do often is helping the costumers find a certain product in store, which is also one of my main jobs.
Working at the Albert Heijn has made me a better listener, because now I have to listen to costumers that have a problem and help them to come to a solution. Also communicating with costumers has made a big difference in my communicative skills. Another thing the Albert Heijn has taught me is working in a team. As a cashier you never work alone, but along your supervisor and your peers.
