Education

The Hague University of Applied Sciences
User Experience Design
2019 - Current

At the Hague University of Applied Sciences, I study User Experience Design. We learn a lot about all different aspects of design. For example we learn how to do research on our users and what you should think of when designing an app or website. We also learn about visuals, and what the most pleasing is to the eyes. We learn how we should look at certain aspects to simplify things or to better them.
While learning, we also work on projects with real clients. An example of this is our Bespoke project with Hallmark.nl. By doing this we see how it will work in the future, how we should listen to the client and to our users at the same time. It is a good way of learning the process of UXD.
